Liver Care

The liver is essential for digesting your food and eliminating toxic substances from the body. Liver problems can be caused by a variety of factors that damage the liver, such as viruses, alcohol use, obesity or genetic factors, autoimmune or Certain prescription medications can also cause liver disease.

Main symptoms are: 1. Yellowish eyes and skin.
2. Severe abdominal pain and swelling.
3. Dark urine and pale stool.
4. Chronic fatigue, Nausea, or vomiting.
5. Loss of appetite, weakness.
6. Jaundice.
7. Chronic constipation.
8. Increased stomach size (building up of fluid in the stomach -Ascites).
9. weight loss, muscle loss.

Over time,chronic liver conditions, causes destruction of liver tissues & lead to scarring (cirrhosis), liver fibrosis which can lead to liver failure, a life-threatening condition.Early natural treatment may give the liver time to heal.
