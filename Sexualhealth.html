When should I visit a Sexologist?

    You should visit a sexologist if you are experiencing one or more of the following issues:

  • Difficulty to orgasm.

  • Experiencing symptoms like vaginal dryness.

  • Questioning sexual identity.

  • Lack of sexual compatibility with your partner.
